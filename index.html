The Stages of Turning in a Late Assignment

By: Adrianne Shay
An Autobiographical Tale based on Recent Events
No computers were harmed in the making of this project.

Prelude: Carefree Joy


You have no obligations, life is yours for the taking! Thank goodness it’s the weekend! I’m gonna sleep in late, hang out with some friends, binge watch some Netflix.
It’s not like I got anything important to do!

Act 1: Remembrance

Oh crap, I have an assignment to do! I almost forgot! It’s due in 2 days.

Intermission: Miscalculation.

Oh it’s due in two days? I’ve got plenty of time.


Act 2: Panic & Despair

THIS ASSIGNMENT IS DUE IS 5 MINUTES AND I HAVE 3 LINES OF CODE. I AM SO EXHAUSTED THAT MY BRAIN IS NOT COMPUTING BASIC FUNCTIONS I SUCK. I’M GOING TO FAIL LAUNCHCODE. MAYBE I SHOULD QUIT.


Act 3: Redemption

It’s okay adrianne. just breath girl. Not all is lost. Regroup, reprioritize, email your tf. Get back on that horse! You go girl! You got this!

Finale: Assignment Submitted

Like a boss!
